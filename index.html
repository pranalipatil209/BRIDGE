Fellowship

BridgeLabz is essentially addressing the problem of employable Fresh Engineers. Apparently 96% of Fresh Engineers are unemployable by the Tech Companies as illustrated in this Economic Times article “Startups find enough funds but not many employable engineers”. This is mainly due to lack of knowledge in Technology Stack and lack of experience in solving real world problems. BridgeLabz fellowship gives aspiring engineers the chance to develop in-depth knowledge in a well defined Tech Stack by developing real-world Apps.


  • work full-time for 3 or 4 Months on a well defined Tech Stack
  • solve real-world Tech Problems
  • mentoring by seasoned techies
  • apply latest technologies, libraries, practices, modular coding on design patterns, git repository, linux env, AWS cloud, etc to solve problems
  • job with Tech Companies partnering with BridgeLabz
  • learn to work hard - 6 days a week and 11 hours a day, putting around 70 hours a week for 3-4 months

    • We essentially focusses on Android & iOS on Native Apps, Web App using Angular & React, Server Side on Java (Spring & Hibernate) & MEAN Stack and Automated Testing using Appium & Selenium Test tool.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Why fellowship program?


    BridgeLabz fellowship program gives aspiring engineers the chance to develop in-depth knowledge in a well defined Tech Stack by developing real-world Apps and employment with Tech Companies.


    What is fellowship program?


    Fellowship Program is a 9 Month Program, 3 month at BridgeLabz and 6 month at Tech Companies. Post the 9 month the Engineers becomes the Employee of the Tech Company.

    In the first 3 months at BridgeLabz the Engineers work 11 hours (8.30AM - 7.30PM) a day and 6 days a week (Monday to Saturday). The Engineer is given a weekly chart and measured on the progress. First two weeks is essentially devoted to getting good understanding on Linux Env, Git Repository and writing around 50 Data Structure, Object Oriented and Algorithmic Programs. Then next couple of weeks the focus is gaining programing knowledge on any one tech stack (Android, iOS, Angular, Java or MEAN). Following this the next couple of weeks is to develop a full stack sample APP using open source libraries, standard coding practices, modular and structured coding on well defined design patterns, Cloud Connectivity, REST Calls, Offline Sync, Async Calls, Websockets, etc. The final 6 weeks is devoted for developing a real world app using the learnings from the sample app.


    What is the minimum qualification needed to fellowship program?


    All the candidates need to be Engineers, with minimum 60% Aggregate, and very comfortable in Coding on Data Structures.


    Who will qualify for fellowship program?


    We look for fresh engineers who are comfortable in coding. If they are comfortable in coding and also have some knowledge in any one of Android, iOS, Angular, Mean or Spring Hibernate Tech Stack, will qualify for fellowship program.


    Will I get Paid for Fellowship Program?


    All Engineers get a stipend of Rs 10,000 per month on the fellowship program of 9 months.


    Is job with Tech Company Guaranteed?


    All Engineers are enrolled into fellowship program only when the Tech Companies gives requirements to BridgeLabz to hire Fresh Engineers.


    What is the benefit of doing Fellowship Program?


    There are several benefits of fellowship program. For starter - job with Tech Company as the enrolment is done only when the Tech Company gives requirement, Second in-depth knowledge in a cutting edge Tech Stack, Third practical experience to develop cloud-aware App to solve real world problems using cutting edge technologies, open source libraries, modular and structured coding, Git Repository, etc.


    Where is the program?


    Fellowship program is currently in Mumbai. We have Fresh Engineers from all over India and also customers from all over India. We have tied up with PG Accomodations for the stay in Mumbai and the stipend paid is sustainable for boarding and lodging.


    What are the work timings in BridgeLabz?


    Monday to Saturday from 8.30 AM to 7.30 PM


    What is a Fellowship Program with Practical Training?


    The Engineers who require further one month Practical Training to qualify for fellowship program are inducted into this 4 month program. Essentially 1 month of practical training and 3 month of fellowship.


    Why full time Fellowship with Practical Training is needed?


    Essentially to serve those Engineers who already have good programming skills but need further grooming on data structure, algorithmic and object oriented programs. Also in practical training the engineers are introduced to one of the Tech Stacks to essentially give a solid start for their fellowship program.


    What is the fee structure for Practical Training and will I get enrolled into fellowship program?


    The Engineers get started with Practical Training by paying just Rs 5,000 initially and then the subsequent 3 month of fellowship, the Engineers will be paid Rs 5000 per month. After that the next 6 month of fellowship, the Engineers will be paid Rs 10,000 per month. This implies the Engineers in Practical Training are auto selected to the fellowship program.


    How is this program different from any other Training programs?


    This program is a live lab where Engineer from day one is expected to code. There is no classroom and there is no curriculum. Through problem solving the concepts are learnt. Further the Engineer will focus and learn a Tech Stack in depth and also solve real world problem to develop a cloud-aware App. Lastly all Engineers in fellowship program have a job with Tech Company as they are enrolled only after the requirements are placed by the Tech Company


    What happens to fellowship engineers after they graduate? What jobs do they get?


    After the 3 month fellowship, the Engineers go on to work with the Tech Company that has signed Contract with BridgeLabz for the next 6 months. After this the Tech Company hires the Engineers into their roles.


    What type of projects will the fellowship engineers get a chance to work on?


    The fellowship engineers work on actual real world problem from the Tech Companies or the IPs being developed by BridgeLabz. The cloud-aware App development spans more than a month with direct interactions with the mentors. The goal is to deliver a product and not a project. We get industry visitors who share their Product World experiences with the fellowship engineers.


    What does the day-to-day look like?


    From morning 8.30 AM to 7.30 PM the fellowship engineers are expected to code. In the beginning it is data structures, later it is live sample app and lastly it is to develop App solving real-world problem statement.


    What tools will fellowship engineers get a chance to learn?


    Essentially the Tech Stack we work on are Android, iOS on Native, Web Apps on Angular and React, Backend Servers on Java, Spring, Hibernate and MEAN stack. All development is done on Linux Environment and for iOS in Mac Machine. We use Heroku and AWS Cloud. We use several other tech stacks like Redis for Caching and Messaging, MQTT for Push Notifications, Git Repository, Dagger for Dependency Injection, Retrofit, Volley & Alamofire for Networking Rest Calls, etc


    What percentage of the fellowship is developing real-world apps?


    100% of fellowship engineers will be involved in developing Cloud Aware Apps solving real world problems.


    What kind of Tech Companies partner with BridgeLabz?


    Most of the Tech Companies partnering with us are Product Companies. They are predominantly established Tech Companies and well funded Startups. Some of the Tech Companies that are partnering with us are CraftsVilla, Zopper, Clover Infotech, ValuePitch, Hexagon Global, GreyTip, Meru Cabs, Finacus Tech, TimesOfMoney, NextEducation, Hullo Inc, etc.
